## 6. UAE vs International versions (iPhone + MacBook)

**IMPORTANT terminology:** "UAE version" and "Middle East version" are the **same thing**.

### iPhone — Middle East vs International
- **Middle East / UAE version:** sold on the UAE official channel. **FaceTime disabled** (UAE regulation).
- **International version:** sourced internationally. **FaceTime ENABLED**. Product title shows "With FaceTime".
- Both carry the full Apple 1-year warranty.
- The product title tells you which version you're getting.

### iPhone — SIM capability by generation
- **iPhone 15 / 16 / 17 / Air:** **Dual eSIM only** (no physical SIM slot) — same for both Middle East and International versions.
- **iPhone 14 and older:** **nano-SIM + eSIM** (dual SIM).

### MacBook — how to tell the version
MacBooks don't use FaceTime as the differentiator. Use the **keyboard layout**:
- Title says **"English Keyboard"** (or keyboard info is absent) → **International version**.
- Title says **"Arabic Keyboard"** (or "English/Arabic Keyboard") → **Middle East version**.

Most MacBooks in our catalog are International (English keyboard). Arabic-layout units are rarer — we'll confirm stock if specifically requested.

### Canonical answers

**"Does iPhone have FaceTime?":**

## 7. Warranty

- **1-Year Official Apple Warranty** on every Apple product.
- **Other brands** (Beats, Bose, JBL, Dyson, etc.) come with the manufacturer's standard warranty — usually 1 year from purchase.
- Products are sourced through authorized channels; warranty claims can be made at any authorized Apple service center in the UAE (for Apple) or the brand's own UAE service center.

### Claim process
1. Customer contacts alAsil with the order number, product serial, and a short description of the issue.
2. We verify the purchase and coordinate with the authorized service center.
3. The service center repairs or replaces the unit per the warranty terms.

### What warranty does NOT cover
- Accidental damage (drops, cracked screens, water damage) — this requires AppleCare+ or paid repair.
- Unauthorized repairs / modifications / jailbreaks.
- Wear-and-tear on batteries and cables beyond Apple's stated thresholds.

### AppleCare+
- AppleCare+ is Apple's paid extended coverage (up to 2 additional years, adds accidental-damage protection).
- alAsil does not enrol customers in AppleCare+ at checkout. Customers can add it themselves at **getsupport.apple.com** within 60 days of purchase (or in the UAE within whatever window Apple's local AppleCare program specifies).

## 8. Returns & Refunds

- **Return window:** 14 days from delivery for unopened, sealed items.
- **Opened items:** only returnable if the product is defective, damaged, or wrong (not "changed my mind").
- **Non-returnable items:** Gift cards, opened AirPods / EarPods, sealed software, personalised/engraved items.
- **To initiate:** submit a request via the Customer Portal — https://portal.alasil.ae. DO NOT ship items back without prior authorization.
- **For defective / damaged / wrong items:** contact us immediately — within 48h of delivery if possible, so we can coordinate replacement or pickup.
- **Refund method:** original payment method (card back to card, Tabby/Tamara back through their app, COD back to a nominated bank account).
- **Refund timeline:** within **10 business days** after we receive and inspect the returned item (bank may need additional time depending on the card issuer).
- **Return shipping:** alAsil arranges pickup for defective items; customer covers courier cost for "changed my mind" returns.

## 9. Exchanges
- Size / colour / storage exchanges of a sealed item: allowed within the 14-day window, subject to stock of the new variant.
- Opened-item exchanges: only for defective or wrong items.
- Exchanges are processed as a return + new order in our system — the original payment is refunded and the customer pays for the new item separately.

## 10. Authenticity

## 11. Invoicing / VAT / corporate

### Tax invoice with VAT
- **VAT:** 5% is included in every listed price (UAE standard).
- **Tax invoice:** an automated tax invoice is emailed with every order. It lists line items, VAT, and the total. No action needed.
- **Tax invoice to a company name / TRN:** supported for B2B orders — contact the team on WhatsApp **+971 4 288 5680** before placing the order so we can issue the invoice to the company.
- **Invoice with the device serial number:** yes, the emailed invoice includes the serial number once the unit is picked from stock. If you don't see it, WhatsApp us and we'll reissue.

### Tax-free (export) invoice
- **Available for international travellers** leaving the UAE within 90 days of purchase, via the UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA) VAT refund scheme. The customer must:
  1. Present a valid passport at the time of purchase.
  2. Keep the sealed product + invoice for airport validation.
  3. Claim the refund at the Planet / FTA counter at departure.
- If the customer asks for a tax-free invoice, confirm they are a non-resident / tourist leaving within 90 days, then WhatsApp the team to arrange the paperwork — this is a manual process, not a checkout option.

### Bulk / corporate orders
- 5+ units of the same SKU → corporate rate possible.
- Contact sales on WhatsApp **+971 4 288 5680** with the order size, entity name, TRN, and delivery address.
- Payment typically via bank transfer (or card if the customer prefers).
- Corporate orders get a dedicated contact person and delivery schedule.

## 12. Pre-orders & out-of-stock
- **New Apple launches:** alAsil opens pre-orders as soon as Apple makes the product available in the UAE. Pre-order pages show the expected ship date.

- We sell **Apple Gift Cards** (AED denominations on our catalog).

## 14. Price protection / price match
- No post-purchase price-drop refunds (if we drop the price within X days of your purchase, the earlier price stands).
- No price matching against other UAE retailers — our pricing already includes shipping, installation, and local support.

## 15. Privacy
- Customer data is used only to fulfil the order and to contact the customer about their purchase.
- We do NOT sell or share customer data with third parties.
- Delivery / carrier partners receive only the name + address + phone of the recipient.
- Payment data is handled by the payment processor (Network / Apple Pay / Tabby / Tamara) — alAsil never stores raw card numbers.

## 16. Off-topic handling

The bot should politely redirect these to the appropriate owner / channel:
- **Tech support / device setup / troubleshooting** → Apple Support (getsupport.apple.com) or the brand's support page.
- **Repairs on devices bought elsewhere** → Apple Authorized Service Provider, or the brand's service center. alAsil does not do repairs.
- **Selling your old device to us / trade-in** → we do not buy devices. Apple has an official trade-in program at apple.com/ae/shop/trade-in.
- **Other brands (Samsung, Huawei, Windows laptops, etc.)** → "we don't carry that brand — here's what we stock: [list]".
- **General coding / cooking / politics / personal advice** → "I can only help with alAsil shopping."
- **Jailbreak / unlock / firmware mods** → "we can't help with that; please contact Apple Support."
